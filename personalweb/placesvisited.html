Welcome

There are a few places that I've visited outside of Massachusetts during my life. These include Cancun, Rhode Island, Florida, and New York.

Cancun


Cancun is a popular tourist spot in Mexico. Many have went here for vacation, including myself. The image above is the beach at Moon Palace Cancun. I have gone on vacation here two times.

Rhode Island


Rhode Island is a coastal city in the US. I have traveled here countless times for their wonderful beaches and good food. Not to mention, Rhode Island is just a few hours away from some parts of Massachusetts.

Florida


The image above shows Cocoa Beach in Florida. I have been to Florida for almost every summer to visit family and enjoy the beaches.

New York


The image above shows Niagara Falls in New York. The Niagara Falls is a tourist location where you can see huge waterfalls. I went to Florida to visit family and see the Niagara Falls.
